
HOW TO DEPLOY OBSIDIAN ECHO – CATHEDRAL OF COGNITION

1. Go to https://github.com and log in.
2. Click the + icon (top right) -> New Repository.
3. Name it: obsidian-echo (or anything you like).
4. Do NOT initialize with README or license.
5. Click Create Repository.

6. On the new repo page, click “Add file” -> “Upload files”.
7. Drag and drop the entire contents of this ZIP (not the ZIP itself) into the upload window.
8. Scroll down and click “Commit changes”.

9. Now go to Settings -> Pages (on the left sidebar).
10. Under Source, choose: `main` branch, `/ (root)` folder.
11. Click Save.
12. After ~30 seconds, your site will be live at:

https://<your-github-username>.github.io/<repo-name>

Enjoy your Cathedral.
